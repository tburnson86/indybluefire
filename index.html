Who We Are

Built in Indiana.
Burned into the Game.

Indy Bluefire is Indianapolis's premier adult volleyball tournament organization — running competitive one-day events on both sand and hard court, year-round, for players of every level.

We believe the best days on the court are the ones you earned. Our events are designed to challenge you, connect you, and bring you back every single time.

Sand or court. Beginner or elite. Solo or squad. You belong here.

How We Play

Tournament Formats

👥
BYOT

Bring Your Own Team. Register as a full squad — pairs, quads, or 6s depending on the event. You built the chemistry, now prove it.

Best For Established groups with regular playing partners. Competitive players who know their lineup.
🤝
BYOP

Bring Your Own Partner. Register as a pair and compete in doubles or quads formats. Great if you have one person but need the rest of your squad.

Best For Doubles players and competitive pairs who don't need a full team to compete.
🎲
Lottery

Register solo and get randomly placed on a team. The ultimate community builder — you'll compete hard with strangers you'll call teammates by noon.

Best For Solo players, newcomers, and anyone who wants to grow their volleyball circle.
